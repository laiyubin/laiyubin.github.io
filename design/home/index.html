SEP Overview

SEP is short for Service Enablement Platform.

Ericsson Composition Engine (ECE), together with Multi-Service Delivery Platform (MSDP) and M2M Data Management (M2M DM) are products in the SEP family. To get a full view of SEP, read SEP Architecture Description 2014.
